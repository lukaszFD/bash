In nano, you can scroll through and edit the file. To exit, press Ctrl + X, then confirm that you want to save your changes.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

nano path/file.log

In vim, you can make changes, scroll and save. To exit edit mode, press Esc, then type :wq and press Enter.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

vim path/file.log

In less you can scroll through the file. To exit, press the q key.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

less path/file.log

cat will display the contents of the file. It can be useful for a quick preview.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

cat path/file.log
